Modern beauty salon with professional stylists and a cozy atmosphere

BeautyTime is a modern beauty salon in the heart of Cologne. We combine professional beauty services with a relaxing atmosphere and personalized care.

Our team uses high-quality products and the latest beauty techniques to provide you with the best results and maximum comfort.

Quality, professionalism, and personalized care are the core values of our salon.

Our Services

Manicure & Pedicure

Professional nail care for hands and feet, including:

  • Cuticle removal
  • Nail filing and shaping
  • High-quality nail polish or gel
  • Relaxing hand and foot scrub
  • Soothing hand and foot massage

Haircut & Styling

Personalized haircuts and styling, including:

  • Consultation on haircut and color
  • Hair washing and care
  • Haircut according to your wishes
  • Styling for everyday or events
  • High-quality care products

Skin Care

Facial treatments for skin care, including:

  • Cleaning and exfoliation
  • Facial massage
  • Moisturizing care
  • Consultation on skin types and products
  • Special treatments for blemishes and wrinkles

Make-up

Professional make-up for any occasion, including:

  • Day make-up
  • Evening and event make-up
  • Consultation on colors and style
  • Skin preparation
  • Fixing for long-lasting hold

Eyebrows & Eyelashes

Perfect eyebrows and eyelashes, including:

  • Eyebrow shaping
  • Tinting eyelashes and eyebrows
  • Lifting or eyelash extensions
  • Nourishing growth serums
  • Personal consultation

Massage

Relaxing massages for body and mind, including:

  • Back and neck massage
  • Full body massage
  • Aromatherapy and oils
  • Relief of tension
  • Improvement of well-being
